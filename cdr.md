## 1. Core publications

| Title | Link | Why it matters |
|-------|------|----------------|
| CDR Primer | https://cdrprimer.org | Consensus-style introduction to carbon removal pathways, economics, risks, and governance. |
| IPCC AR6 WGIII – Chapter 12 (CDR) | https://www.ipcc.ch/report/ar6/wg3/ | Scientific backbone for why, how much, and under what scenarios CDR is needed. |
| State of Carbon Dioxide Removal | https://www.stateofcdr.org | Annual global stocktake of CDR deployment, research, and policy. |
| National Academies – Negative Emissions Technologies (2019) | https://nap.nationalacademies.org/catalog/25259 | Rigorous assessment of feasibility, costs, and research needs. |
| Frontier CDR Landscape | https://frontierclimate.com | Clear overview of emerging CDR companies and pathways. |
| UNEP Emissions Gap Report (CDR sections) | https://www.unep.org/resources/emissions-gap-report | Shows how CDR fits into global mitigation shortfalls. |

## 2. Pathway-specific deep dives

| Pathway | Key resource | Summary |
|---------|--------------|---------|
| Direct Air Capture (DAC) | IEA DAC Report | Technical overview of DAC chemistry, energy needs, and deployment pathways. |
| BECCS | UK CCC BECCS analysis | Explores sustainability constraints, land use, and realistic deployment levels. |
| Enhanced Rock Weathering | Oxford Geoengineering Programme | Scientific basis, field trials, and MRV challenges. |
| Ocean Alkalinity Enhancement | Ocean Visions Roadmap | Engineering, ecological risks, and monitoring frameworks. |
| Biochar | IBI Biochar Guidelines | Practical, near-term CDR with strong co-benefits. |

## 3. Civil society, NGOs, and academic centres

| Organisation | Link | Role |
|--------------|------|------|
| Carbon180 | https://carbon180.org | US NGO shaping federal CDR policy and land-sector removals. |
| RMI – Carbon Removal | https://rmi.org | Market design, procurement, and industrial decarbonisation. |
| OpenAir Collective | https://openaircollective.cc | Volunteer network advancing open-source CDR policy and tech. |
| ClimateWorks – CDR | https://climateworks.org | Philanthropic coordination and global CDR strategy. |
| Oxford Net Zero | https://netzeroclimate.org | Research on net-zero governance and CDR integrity. |
| Energy Futures Initiative | https://energyfuturesinitiative.org | Techno-economic analysis of DAC and CO₂ infrastructure. |
| Carbon Removal Advocacy Europe | https://carbon-removal.eu | EU-focused policy and standards development. |

## 4. Geography-specific roadmaps

| Region | Key roadmap | Why it’s useful |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| United States | DOE Carbon Negative Shot | Defines cost targets, R&D priorities, and deployment pathways. |
| European Union | EU Industrial Carbon Management Strategy | First EU-wide CDR + CCS strategy with targets and governance. |
| United Kingdom | UK Net Zero Strategy – GGR chapter | Framework for engineered removals, BECCS, and biochar. |
| Canada | Canada Carbon Management Strategy | Focus on DAC hubs and geological storage. |
| Global South | ClimateWorks Global South CDR assessment | Highlights equity, capacity building, and differentiated pathways. |

## 5. Policy and governance

| Topic | Resource | Summary |
|-------|----------|---------|
| MRV | Puro.earth methodologies | Practical standards for permanence, leakage, and quantification. |
| Carbon markets & integrity | ICVCM Core Carbon Principles | Framework for high-integrity carbon credits. |
| Environmental justice & CDR | Carbon180 EJ framework | Ensures CDR deployment benefits communities and avoids harm. |
| Infrastructure & storage | Princeton Net-Zero America CO₂ transport analysis | Pipeline and storage build-out requirements. |

## 6. Industry, startups, and deployment

| Category | Resource | Why it matters |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| Company landscape | Frontier CDR portfolio | Shows real companies delivering measurable removals. |
| Procurement | Microsoft carbon removal reports | Transparent, high-quality procurement case studies. |
| Techno-economics | IEA CDR cost curves | Grounded cost projections and deployment constraints. |

## 7. “If you only have 3 hours” starter pack

1. **CDR Primer** – Chapters 1–3  
2. **State of CDR** – Executive summary  
3. **Carbon180 – CDR 101**  
4. **Frontier CDR landscape**  
5. One pathway deep dive (DAC or biochar)  
6. One policy piece (ICVCM or DOE Carbon Negative Shot)
