Here are some expansions:



\[
x + \frac{x^3}{6} + \frac{3x^5}{40} + \frac{5x^7}{112} + \cdots
\]





\[
1 + x + x^2 + \frac{2}{3}x^3 + \frac{1}{2}x^4 + \cdots
\]





\[
1 + x \ln(a) + \frac{(x \ln a)^2}{2!} + \frac{(x \ln a)^3}{3!} + \cdots
\]
