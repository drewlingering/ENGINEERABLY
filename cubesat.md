## üß† Key Considerations

- **Launch Access**: Most individuals use **rideshare brokers** (e.g., SpaceX Rideshare, Exolaunch, Nanoracks).
- **Regulatory Compliance**:
  - **Frequency licensing** (e.g., Ofcom, ITU)
  - **Launch approvals** (e.g., UK Civil Aviation Authority)
- **Technical Requirements**:
  - Radiation-tolerant electronics
  - Thermal and power management
  - Attitude control (if needed for pointing or imaging)
- **Open-Source Ecosystem**:
  - [LibreCube](https://librecube.net/)
  - [SatNOGS](https://satnogs.org/)
  - [PocketQube Shop](https://www.pocketqubeshop.com/)

## üåç Trends Enabling Individuals

- **PocketQubes** (smaller than CubeSats) reduce launch mass and cost.
- **Crowdfunding** (e.g., Kickstarter) has enabled several successful missions.
- **Educational kits** like *MySat* and *PiSat* allow prototyping and testing on Earth.
